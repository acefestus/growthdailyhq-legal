Privacy Policy

GrowthDailyHQ
Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize GrowthDailyHQ to access your social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok, X), we receive basic account information such as your username and account ID through OAuth2 authorization. We do not collect passwords.

2. How We Use Information

Account access is used solely for publishing content (videos, posts) to your authorized social media platforms. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the application server and are not transmitted to external services. Upload logs are stored locally for operational purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

GrowthDailyHQ integrates with YouTube (Google), TikTok, and X (Twitter) APIs. Your use of these platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain authorization tokens only as long as needed to provide the service. You may revoke access at any time through your social media platform settings.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage of API credentials and tokens.

7. Children's Privacy

GrowthDailyHQ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
